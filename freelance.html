how it works

STEP 1

Choose from my list, or schedule a call with me to talk about your project needs. Depending on the size, some projects require more than one day, or less than a day.

STEP 2

Schedule a time that works best for you and pay your deposit. We will collaborate closely, so you’ll need to be available by text, phone, or video chat on the day you selected.

Once you’ve scheduled your day and paid your deposit, you’ll receive an email with a checklist of work to complete before our meeting.

On the day of your intensive, we will collaborate through a combo of text, phone, or video chat to complete your project!

STEP 5

After our project is finished, I will be available for 30 days of support to help with tweaks, training, or questions.
